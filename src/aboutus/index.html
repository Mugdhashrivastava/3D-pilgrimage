About Us

Mission

To implement a virtual pilgramage experience for anyone siiting in any part of the world . People are now just one click away from witnessing beautiful places and pilgramages. You can experience real time pilgramage and tourism through our website. The person just have to simply visit to our website and he can travel to any place of his choice. He can view the place from outside as well as can navigate inside. All the steps regarding how to navigate inside the place would be described . He will get a demo of how one can visit the places by our website.

Vision

To help each and every individual at corners of the world to experience our culture and heritage through our website. It won't only enhance the tourism in our country but will help millions of people who can't visit these holy and immaculate places due to some physiological or personal reasons. We are currently working on expansion of pilgramages model in our website by which we will create the models of every possible town and city.


Technology stack

  • 3.js will be used for 3D modelling of the virtual pilgrimages.
  • ReactJs will be used in the front end.
  • NodeJs, ExpressJs will be used in the back end.
  • MongoDb will be the database to store information.
